    Product Description
    Incredible Unisoc T612 Processor

    The Unisoc T612 processor, which powers the realme C30, provides an exceptionally smooth working environment and potent performance. You can effortlessly switch between apps with ease and complete more work in less time without any stutter.

    Robust Battery

    You can seamlessly chat, stream movies, and play online games on the enormous battery-powered realme C30 which provides uninterrupted battery life, allowing you to enhance your productivity without having to worry about running short of power.

    New Suitcase Design

    Imitating the iconic geometry of the C35, this C30 smartphone flaunts a striped Suitcase design, which is intended to bring travel and technology together, thus making it a delight for you to carry along your mobile whenever you are on the move.

    Terrific 8 MP AI Camera

    The realme C30 with its outstanding 8 MP AI Camera offers a wide range of photographic modes, such as Time-lapse, Panorama, Macro, Night Pro, and many more, enabling you to cherish and applaud the marvel of all magnificent things in the world.

    3 Card Slot Advantage

    With the realme C30's dual SIM card compatibility and up to 1 TB of memory expansion through a microSD card slot, you can switch between them as you like and store additional games, pictures, and data with ease.

    Ultra Saving Mode Reliability

    This smartphone has a remarkable Ultra Saving Mode that enables you to continue working on your tasks for a prolonged period of time even with just about 5% power left and conclude the day with a grin.

    Splendid 5 MP Selfie Camera

    The 5 MP selfie camera on the realme C30 enables you to take stunning selfies which allows you to gain popularity on your social media handles, thus, helping you in becoming a celebrity among your peers.
